Top 5 Things to Do in Paris

  1. Eiffel Tower

    The iconic symbol of Paris and a must-visit landmark offering breathtaking views of the city.

  2. Louvre Museum

    Home to the famous Mona Lisa, the Louvre is the world's largest art museum.

  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral

    A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture located on Île de la Cité.

  4. Sacré-Cœur Basilica

    This white-domed basilica sits atop Montmartre and offers stunning views of Paris.
