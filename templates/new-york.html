Top 5 Things to Do in New York

  1. 1. Statue of Liberty

    The iconic symbol of New York and a must-visit landmark offering breathtaking views of the city.

  2. 2. Central Park

    An urban oasis in the heart of Manhattan, perfect for picnics, walks, and scenic views.

  3. 3. Brooklyn Bridge

    A historic suspension bridge offering stunning views of Manhattan and Brooklyn.

  4. 4. Rockefeller Center

    A must-see destination, especially during the holiday season, featuring ice skating and city views.
